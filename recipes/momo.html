Momo recipie


"Mo:Mo" (or Momo) is a popular Nepali dumpling dish, often filled with spiced meat or vegetables. Here's a simple recipe to make traditional Momos with chicken filling:
Ingedients
For the Dough:

  • All-purpose flour: 2cups
  • Water: 1/ 2 cup
  • Salt: 1/ 2 tsp
For the Filling:
  • Ground chicken: 1lb (or minced vegetables for veg momos)
  • Onion: 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 4-5 cloves, minced
  • Ginger: finely chopped
  • Cilantro: finely chopped
  • Soy sauce: 1tbsp
  • Sesame oil: 1tsp (optional)
  • Salt: to taste
  • Ground pepper: 1/ 2 tsp
For the Dipping Sauce:
  • Tomatoes: 2, roasted or boiled
  • Garlic: 3 cloves
  • Red chilies: to taste
  • Roasted sesame seeds: 1tbsp
  • Cilantro: a handful
  • Salt: to taste
  • Lemon juice: 1tsp

Instructions
  1. Prepare the Dough:
    1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Gradually add water and knead into a smooth, elastic dough.
    2. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Make the Filling:
    1. In a bowl, mix the chicken (or vegetables), onion, garlic, ginger, green chilies, cilantro, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper until well combined.
    2. Adjust seasoning as per your taste.
  3. Shape the Momos:
    1. Divide the dough into small, equal-sized balls.
    2. Roll each ball into a thin, round wrapper (about 3 inches in diameter).
    3. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each wrapper.
    4. Fold and pinch the edges to seal, creating your preferred shape (crescent, round, or pleated).
  4. Cook the Momos:
    1. Prepare a steamer by greasing the tray lightly with oil to prevent sticking.
    2. Arrange the momos on the tray, leaving space between them.
    3. Steam for 10-12 minutes, or until the wrapper becomes translucent, and the filling is fully cooked.
  5. Make the Dipping Sauce:
    1. Blend roasted tomatoes, garlic, red chilies, sesame seeds, cilantro, salt, and lemon juice into a smooth sauce.
    2. Adjust seasoning and texture with water if needed.
  6. Serve:
    Serve the momos hot with the dipping sauce on the side.
